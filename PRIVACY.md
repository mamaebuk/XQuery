# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 14, 2026**

## Data Collection
X Query Search **does not collect, store, or transmit any user information** to external servers. All data (including search templates, favorites, and settings) is stored locally on your device using the browser's `chrome.storage.local` API.

## Data Usage
- **Search Templates & Favorites**: Build queries and save them locally for quick access. This data never leaves your browser.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (e.g., language, theme) are stored locally.

## Third-Party Services
This extension operates entirely client-side and does not use any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising networks.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/jarodise/XQuery).
